# event-managing-website
An event manager is a professional who plans, organizes, and executes various functions—such as corporate conferences, weddings, or festivals—by managing all details from venue selection and vendor negotiations to on-site execution and post-event evaluations
They oversee logistics, vendors, and staff for corporate conferences, weddings, festivals, and more. Their role covers strategic planning, marketing, on-site management, and post-event evaluation.
